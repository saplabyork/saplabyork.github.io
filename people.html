Chandan Narayan Director

Chandan is an Associate Professor of Linguistics in the Department of Languages, Literatures, and Linguistics at York. He's also a member of the Graduate Faculty in Psychology. His areas of expertise are infant and adult speech perception, acoustic phonetics, sound change, and the speech-cognition interface. He has degrees from UC Berkeley and the University of Michigan.
