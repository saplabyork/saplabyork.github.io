We're Linguistics, Psychology, and Cognitive Science students at York University trying to answer questions about the nature of speech processing, its relation to linguistic structure, language history, cognitive development, and its interactions with other domains of cognition. We conduct acoustics and speech perception experiments using various active and passive methods at our lab in 127 Founders College and the Phonetics Studio in Ross. If you're interested in volunteering in the lab or would like to participate in our studies, please contact us.
