We're Linguists, Psychologists, and Cognitive Scientists at York University trying to answer questions about the nature of speech processing, its relation to linguistic structure and cognitive development, and its interactions with other domains of cognition. We conduct speech perception experiments using various active and passive methods at our lab in 127 Founders College. We also collaborate with the Cognitive Development Lab and Sensory Biophysics Lab at York as well as the UT Scarborough Linguistics Lab. If you're interested in volunteering in the lab or would like to participate in our studies, please contact us.
